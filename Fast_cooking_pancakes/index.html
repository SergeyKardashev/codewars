Fast cooking pancakes

You need to cook pancakes, but you are very hungry. As known, one needs to fry a pancake one minute on each side. What is the minimum time you need to cook n pancakes, if you can put on the frying pan only m pancakes at a time?
n and m are positive integers between 1 and 1000.
